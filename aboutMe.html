Problem solver
Having worked in several industries, I've encountered a wide range of different problems. The biggest takeaway from my experience is that every problem has its own unique complexities, no matter how simple it appears on the surface. I market myself as a web developer because that is what my sillset is best suited for at the present. With any software I develop, I focus on the real world problem I am trying to solve.
Always learning
I push myself to try new tools and languages whenever I see a real use for it. As a professional, I take every opportunity to learn a new skill. If a project comes up that requires a library no one knows how to use, I will volunteer to learn it. There are few things I enjoy more than seeing progress after working hard to develop a new capacity.
Active listener
Listening is a skill that requires practice. It is much easier to ask someone to clarify something I didn't quite understand than it is to rewrite code after assuming I knew what the person is talking about. I try to start every conversation from the assumption the other person knows something I don't.
